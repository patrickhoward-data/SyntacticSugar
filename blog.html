Zip Codes are Racist

By Patrick Howard
May 24th, 2022

This post was/is inspired by the Instagram acount @segregation_by_design.
I lived in South Central Los Angeles for a time and had front row seat to the phenomenon of "White Flight".
My original thought is to compile the intersection of zip codes and area codes. and then cross reference that against income per capita within those defined postal/area codes.
We give these numbers away freely, numbers that define us without us being aware of the impact it maybe having on us.
